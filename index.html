Publications

Research Publications

One published paper and some manuscripts moving through preparation, centered on Aspen HYSYS process simulation for sustainable biodiesel production.

Published

Steady-State Simulation and Energy Analysis of a Two-Stage Heterogeneous Catalytic Process for Biodiesel Production from High-FFA Waste Cooking Oil Using Aspen HYSYS

Journal of Engineering and Applied Science · 73, Article 235 · 2026

Bhurse, T. & Sett, A.

Key result: A two-stage SHER (acid esterification) + waste-mussel-shell CaO (base transesterification) system achieved 98.7% FFA conversion and 99.9% triglyceride conversion, with heat integration cutting specific energy use from 3.08 to as low as 1.66 MJ/kg.

🔗 DOI: 10.1186/s44147-026-01090-8
Preprint

Simulation of Biodiesel from Waste Cooking Oil (WCO) in Aspen HYSYS and Its Optimization Study

ChemRxiv · Posted 15 Oct 2025 · Internationally Indexed

Bhurse, T. G.

Key result: A continuous two-stage CSTR system (65 °C, 6:1 methanol-to-oil ratio, 2 wt% KOH) reached ~99% triglyceride conversion, >99% product purity, and ~98% methanol recovery via distillation.

In Revision (with Faculty)

Defining a Feasible Industrial Operating Window for Waste Cooking Oil Biodiesel via Integrated Reaction–Separation Simulation

Co-author: Dr. Ayantika Sett

Bhurse, T. & Sett, A.

Key result: ~96% FFA/triglyceride conversion within a Feasible Industrial Operating Window (FIOW) of 60–63 °C and 6.5–7.5:1 methanol-to-oil ratio; a Damköhler number of 3.77 confirms kinetically dominant operation.

Revision

Solar-Thermal Intensification of Biodiesel Methanol Recovery: High-Resolution Thermodynamic Mapping and CSP Sizing for a Low-Carbon Circular Bioeconomy

Bioresource Technology Reports · Manuscript No. BITEB-D-26-01036

Bhurse, T.

Key result: A global energetic optimum of 2292 kJ/kg at 89 °C; at the recommended 105 °C operating point, 80.5% methanol recovery at >99.89 wt% purity, ~136 m² CSP collector sizing, and a 2.45-year payback.

In Preparation

Purity-Constrained Operating Cost Minimum for a Single-Stage Methanol Flash Unit in Decentralized Biodiesel Production

Co-author: Dr. Sushma Chakraborty

Bhurse, T. & Chakraborty, S.

Key result: A 130–136 °C safe operating window for a 1 TPD plant delivers >90% solvent recovery and >99 wt% purity, keeping annualized costs within 2% of the mathematical minimum (~2,230 USD/yr).

In Preparation · Review

Thermal Energy Storage for Energy Systems: A Short Review of Performance Limitations and Research Gaps

Co-author: Dr. Ayantika Sett

Sett, A. & Bhurse, T.

Key result: Reviews sensible, latent, and thermochemical storage limitations, highlighting the gap between India's >750 GW solar potential and ~101 MW of installed CSP–TES capacity.
